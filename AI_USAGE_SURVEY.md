## Survey Administration Guide

### For Survey Administrators

**Distribution:**
1. Send via email to all team members
2. Post in Slack/Teams with deadline
3. Mention in team meetings
4. Send reminder 3 days before deadline
5. Target: 80%+ response rate

**Timeline:**
- Send survey: [Date]
- Reminder: [Date, 3-5 days later]
- Final reminder: [Date, 1 day before close]
- Close survey: [Date, 7-10 days after launch]
- Analyze results: [Date, within 1 week]
- Share findings: [Date, 1-2 weeks after close]

**Tools:**
- **Recommended**: Google Forms (easy, free, integrates with Sheets)
- **Alternatives**: Typeform (better UX), Microsoft Forms, SurveyMonkey

**Analysis Plan:**
1. **Quantitative Analysis**:
   - Calculate response rates by team
   - Create charts for multiple-choice questions
   - Identify most-used tools
   - Calculate time spent on tasks
   - Measure comfort levels and attitudes

2. **Qualitative Analysis**:
   - Code open-ended responses into themes
   - Identify common pain points
   - Extract specific use case ideas
   - Note interesting quotes for reports

3. **Segmentation**:
   - Analyze by role (Sales vs Support vs Ops)
   - Compare by experience level
   - Identify "power users" vs "non-users"
   - Find early adopters vs skeptics

4. **Outputs**:
   - Executive summary (2 pages)
   - Detailed findings report (10-15 pages)
   - Presentation for team sharing
   - Input for AI strategy roadmap

**Privacy & Ethics:**
- Make individual responses confidential
- Only share aggregate data publicly
- Use optional name field for follow-ups only
- Store data securely
- Delete after analysis period (or as per policy)
