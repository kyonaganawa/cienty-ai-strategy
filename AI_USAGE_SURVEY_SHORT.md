### Analysis Quick Guide

**Quantitative Analysis (Questions 1-11):**

1. **Current AI Adoption:**
   - % using AI tools vs not using
   - Most popular tools (Q2)
   - Time spent per week (Q4)
   - Breakdown by role (Q1)

2. **Barriers Analysis (Q5):**
   - Top 3 barriers to adoption
   - Segment by current users vs non-users

3. **Opportunity Mapping (Q6-8):**
   - Most common time-consuming tasks
   - Top desired AI capabilities by role
   - Customer-facing opportunities

4. **Concerns & Readiness (Q9-11):**
   - Main concerns about AI
   - Comfort levels
   - Training interest

**Qualitative Analysis (Q3, Q6, Q12):**

1. **Code responses into themes:**
   - Task categories (communication, data analysis, admin, etc.)
   - Pain points (repetitive work, lack of information, manual processes)
   - Creative ideas (prioritize by feasibility + impact)

2. **Extract quotes:**
   - Powerful examples for presentations
   - Specific use cases to prioritize

3. **Identify patterns:**
   - Common tasks across multiple people
   - Role-specific needs
   - Quick wins vs long-term opportunities
